EXHIBIT_A // THE_ADLER_LOG // 1917

The mist in the trenches wasn't gas. It was static. We found Lieutenant Adler in the mud, his hands burned black to the bone, clutching this radio log.

"I patched the switchboard. I plugged the first cord into Jack 2."

"The current screamed, arcing across Jack 3 and burning out Jack 4."

"I dropped the cord and jammed a grounded wire into Jack 9. The interference pulled it violently straight down to Jack 13."

"The board caught fire, the flame tracing across 14 and 15, finally melting Jack 16 into slag."

"I am drawing the shape of the sound. God help us."
